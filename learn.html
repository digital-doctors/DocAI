DocAI is a web application designed to combat global malnutrition. Initially developed as a prototype for the Imagine Cup Junior submission, it currently operates with limited input and output capabilities. However, its ambition extends beyond its current state. The ultimate goal is to evolve it into a comprehensive health monitor powered by AI resources from Azure and OpenAI.


In its advanced form, DocAI will offer personalized feedback to users by analyzing data synced from the Samsung Health app. By harnessing the capabilities of AI and integrating with existing health platforms, DocAI aims to revolutionize how individuals manage their health and nutrition. This innovative approach not only addresses the pressing issue of malnutrition but also provides users with tailored insights to optimize their overall well-being. With its commitment to leveraging technology for social good, DocAI is poised to make a significant impact on the global health landscape.
